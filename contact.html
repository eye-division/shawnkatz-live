37A Lansdowne Place Brighton and Hove, East Sussex BN3 1HF

Telephone: 07766 707531 or 01273 875961

Directions

The location on Lansdowne Place is between Western Road and the seafront, a few doors down from the Starbucks. It is approximately a 1-mile walk from the Hove train station and a 1.5 mile walk from Brighton main train station.

Bus lines 5, 25, & 49 which depart from both Hove & Brighton stations, stop on Western Road. (Line 1 stops on Western Road as well but only goes by the Brighton station).

If you are driving, there is on street parking in zoned areas for £2 an hour, £4.20 for two hours, but please leave a few minutes to find parking, as it can be busy. Most of the payment for parking is now pay by phone, which needs to be registered and set up beforehand by going to www.paybyphone.co.uk or by calling 01273-458458.
